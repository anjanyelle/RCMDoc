What Is This Application?

Simple Explanation

When you visit a hospital, a complex behind-the-scenes process kicks off. The hospital must verify your insurance, document your treatment, generate billing codes, submit claims to insurance, and collect payment — all before your bill is settled.

Traditionally this takes 60–90 days and is full of manual errors. Our application automates the entire pipeline and reduces it to 5–7 days with 95%+ accuracy.

The Full RCM Cycle — At a Glance

Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) is the complete financial process a hospital uses to manage patient treatment, insurance billing, and payment collection. It starts the moment a patient enters the facility and ends only when every last dollar is collected from insurance and patient. The cycle spans provider credentialing → patient registration → insurance verification → prior authorization → clinical documentation → medical coding → charge capture → claim submission → adjudication → payment posting → denial management → patient billing → collections.

Specialized Billing Types Supported

US Healthcare billing is not one-size-fits-all. Our system handles every major payer type and billing pathway.

Medicare & Medicare Advantage

  • Traditional Medicare Parts A, B, C, D
  • Medicare Advantage (Part C) plan-specific rules
  • Risk adjustment & HCC coding support
  • MIPS / MACRA quality reporting
  • ABN generation for non-covered services
  • Medicare Secondary Payer (MSP) rules enforced

Medicaid & Managed Medicaid

  • State-specific Medicaid fee schedules (all 50 states)
  • Managed Medicaid MCO billing rules
  • Medicaid as payer of last resort enforced
  • Third Party Liability (TPL) tracking
  • Presumptive eligibility & Medicaid enrollment screening
  • Medicaid timely filing calendar per state

Workers Compensation Billing

  • First Report of Injury filing & tracking
  • State-specific WC fee schedules
  • Causation & relatedness review documentation
  • Independent Medical Examination (IME) tracking
  • Separate WC billing pathway from health insurance

Auto Insurance / Motor Vehicle Accidents

  • PIP (Personal Injury Protection) billing
  • MedPay claim submission
  • Hospital lien filing & management
  • Subrogation rights tracking
  • Auto insurance → health insurance fallback flow

TRICARE / Military Healthcare

  • TRICARE Prime, Select, Reserve Select, For Life
  • Regional contractor routing by geography
  • Separate prior authorization process
  • TRICARE For Life as Medicare supplement (secondary payer)

Telehealth Billing

  • POS 02 (telehealth) and POS 10 (patient at home)
  • Modifier GT and 95 per payer rules
  • Audio-only vs video telehealth codes
  • Store-and-forward billing (dermatology, radiology)
  • Payer-specific telehealth rule library

Pharmacy / Drug Billing (J Codes)

  • HCPCS J-code billing for infused / injectable drugs
  • Drug wastage billing with Modifier JW
  • 340B Drug Discount Program tracking
  • Buy-and-bill reimbursement margin tracking
  • Specialty drug prior authorization workflows

Behavioral Health & Substance Use

  • MHPAEA mental health parity compliance monitoring
  • Psychotherapy add-on code billing
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) billing
  • 42 CFR Part 2 confidentiality rules for SUD records
  • Residential and outpatient SUD program billing

Compliance & Regulatory Framework

Compliance Program Elements (OIG)

  • Written policies & procedures for all billing activities
  • Designated Compliance Officer & Compliance Committee
  • Staff training & education programs
  • Confidential reporting hotline (non-retaliation)
  • Disciplinary standards applied consistently
  • Regular internal audits & monitoring
  • Prompt response to detected problems & voluntary self-disclosure

Government Audit Bodies

  • RAC — Recovery Audit Contractors (Medicare overpayments)
  • MAC — Medicare Administrative Contractors
  • OIG — Office of Inspector General (fraud & abuse)
  • ZPIC — Zone Program Integrity Contractors
  • State Medicaid Audit Agencies
  • False Claims Act — 3x penalty + per-claim fines
  • Corporate Integrity Agreements (CIA) tracked in system

Coding Compliance Rules

  • NCCI Edits — prevent unbundling of procedures
  • LCD Rules — local Medicare coverage determinations
  • NCD Rules — national Medicare coverage rules
  • MUE Limits — medically unlikely edit caps per day
  • Upcoding detection & prevention controls
  • Downcoding identification for revenue recovery

Patient Protection Rules

  • No Surprises Act — balance billing prohibition
  • ABN — Advance Beneficiary Notice for Medicare non-covered services
  • Price Transparency — machine-readable charge files published
  • Good Faith Estimates — pre-service cost estimates
  • MHPAEA — mental health parity compliance
  • 42 CFR Part 2 — SUD record confidentiality
  • Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA)
